I grew up in Massamá, Portugal a suburb of Lisbon. My first jobs were as a chef, which took me to London in my late teens to work in a 2 Michelin-starred restaurant and the 4th best in the world at the time. Eventually, I left hospitality to pursue another passion I had kept on the back burner, technology. I went to Singapore where I attended college and graduated in Computer Science. Since then I worked in the world of Big Data as a Data Engineer and Scientist, which instilled in me a passion for mathematics and high-performance computing.

My role is to design tools for A/B experimentation, statistics, large-scale data processing and maintain several data pipelines for these applications.

  • Location: Singapore
  • Role: Data Engineer
  • Tenure: June 2019 until present

MobileWalla

MobileWalla collects data from mobile devices, which is then used to create segments for targeted advertising and audience exploration. During my time at MobileWalla I was responsible for developing several high-throughput data pipelines and optimise the resource allocation for these clusters. In some cases the cost of the AWS’s cluster reduced as much as 91%.

  • Location: Singapore
  • Role: Data Engineer
  • Tenure: June 2019 to June 2020

Utopia

Utopia is one of the top SAP’s extensions, and their preferred partner for Master Data Governance. At Utopia I worked in the Data Science Research team where we applied the latest machine learning methods to solve long-standing industry issues. Personally I worked on information extraction from digitised documents my proposed solution merged Deep Learning (i.e. CNNs) with Clustering methods (i.e. KNN) and image processing (i.e OpenCV).

  • Location: Bangalore, India
  • Role: Data Scientist Intern
  • Tenure: December 2018 to May 2019
