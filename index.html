I grew up in Massamá, Portugal a suburb of Lisbon. My first jobs were as a chef, which took me to London in my late teens to work in a 2 Michelin-starred restaurant and the 4th best in the world at the time. Eventually, I left hospitality to pursue another passion I had kept on the back burner, technology. I went to Singapore where attended college and graduated in Computer Science. Since then I have worked in the world of Big Data as a Data Engineer and Scientist, which instilled in me a passion for mathematics and high-performance computing.
